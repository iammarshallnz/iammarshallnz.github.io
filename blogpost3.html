Air O Naut

A simulated space deveopment adventure game

Development Ideas

I want to create a game similar to that of Frostpunk, which is a winter survival game where you control how the society functions. I want something very close to this style of gameplay, except the players exist on a planet (this is most likely mars), and instead of a story that has a begining and end the player has to deal with random scenarios.

I want to keep to simple art as it will make it easier to expand the development with out having to delay for art that I will make. In the future I can always change the art up to create a different looking experience. More of a concern I feel is the music, I feel that there is two ways for the music to go. Space beepy boopy, or some kind of music that induces isolation.

Internal discussion section

After experimenting with VR games and looking more into the development of VR games, I found my-self losing interest in what I feel is a gimmick for video game development. VR has a place in many different industries ( e.g. Medical, industrial, learning ), but its place in video games feels increasingly like it is slipping from a foothold. I accept that in the future my opinion may change, but at the moment I feel like pursuing other game development areas.
