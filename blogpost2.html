Pump Up

An VR exercise experience

Learning points

For this game I wanted to create a simple but fun program in which the player swings around dumbbells at weighted plates, each time the player hits these plates they gain points. The longer the plate has be survived the less points. To encourage movement from the player, the plates are designed to come out of random points around the player. At specific points in the song the plates will only come from high up making the player jump, or when the plates are low the player has to squat down.

To keep the player engaged I hand picked the perfect song that will keep the players body moving for long enough to be considered light exercise.





Internal discussion section

The development of this game was much more efficient and the simple design means that the player is more focused on the gameplay. I am very fond of how this game developed during creation.
